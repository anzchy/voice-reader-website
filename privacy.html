Introduction

Voice Reader ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Voice Reader is an EPUB reader with text-to-speech functionality. The app is designed with privacy in mindâ€”your reading data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Books: EPUB files you import into the app
  • Reading Progress: Current position, bookmarks, and highlights
  • App Settings: TTS configuration, reading preferences
  • Notes: Any notes you add to your books

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Location data

Third-Party TTS Services

Important: When you use text-to-speech, the text content is sent to your configured TTS provider to generate audio. This is necessary for the app's core functionality.

Voice Reader supports multiple TTS providers. You choose which one to use:

Provider Data Sent Privacy Policy
Microsoft Azure Speech Text content for audio synthesis Microsoft Privacy
Speechify API Text content for audio synthesis Speechify Privacy
Piper (Self-hosted) Text to your own server Your server, your control
Custom API Text to your configured endpoint Your responsibility

We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of your chosen TTS provider. You provide your own API credentials, and we do not have access to your TTS accounts.

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All app data is stored locally on your iOS device using standard iOS storage mechanisms.
  • API Credentials: Your TTS API keys are stored securely using iOS Keychain or UserDefaults.
  • No Cloud Sync: Voice Reader does not sync data to any cloud service.
  • Device Security: Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, etc.).

Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View Data: All your data is visible within the app
  • Delete Data: Delete individual books, notes, or the entire app
  • Export: Your EPUB files remain in standard format
  • Change TTS Provider: Switch providers anytime in Settings

To delete all Voice Reader data, simply uninstall the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Voice Reader does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app does not require account creation or collect user data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
